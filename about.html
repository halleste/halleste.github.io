Affects on Marine Life Post Spill

Within six months after the oil spill, 8,000 birds, sea turtles, and marine mammals were either killed or injured due to oil contact.

Oil acts in many ways to kill or harm species. Here are some of the ways:


  • Oil injestion: poisens animals and causes ulcer and internal bleeding

  • Oil body coating: destroys insulation for animals with fur, killing such animals from hypothermia

  • Oil coating bird wings: Inability for birds to fly and decreases their boyancy and ability to keep stable body temperature
