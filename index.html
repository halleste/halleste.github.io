The Deepwater Horizon Oil Spill

Quick Facts?

  • On April 20, 2010, an explosion on the Deepwater Horizon oil rig (located in the Gulf of Mexico )

  • It is known as the largest marine oil spill in history.

  • 134 million gallons of oil were released into the Gulf of Mexico

  • The fire lasted 36 hours

  • 87 days went by before the rig sank

  • 1,300 miles of shoreline were contaminated with oil

  • On April 22, the ring sank, terminating the explosions and fires
