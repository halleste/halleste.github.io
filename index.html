Pill bugs

Let's talk about pill bugs!
Also known as rollie pollies!

What is their ancestry?

They were introduced from Europe and descended from water crustacians

What is their lifestyle

  • Rollie Pollies are nocternal animals that live in dark humid places like under logs
  • They decay plant leaves and other materials
  • They are benificial for ecosystems
  • They can roll up into a ball as a defense mechanism
