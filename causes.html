What errors caused the Oil Rig to explode?

As a result of British Patroleum's (BP) misreading data and poor judegent-making, a mixture of the following errors resulted in the Oil Rig explosion

  • Dodgy cement

  • Valve failure

  • Pressure test

  • misinterpreted data

  • Leak not spotted soon enough

  • Valve failure no. 2 (8 minutes before the explosion)

  • Overwhelmed separator

  • No gas alarm

  • No battery for BOP
